Classic Waffles

Description

There are waffles... and then there are your grandma's waffles! Enjoy the taste of home sweet home with this recipe - nothing beats the classics!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
