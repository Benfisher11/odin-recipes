Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread is so delicious you won't even believe it. Read below to learn how to create banana bread so moist and delicous, you will become the talk of party!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
